Tomato and beef pasta ingredients

Minced beef 200g pasta 400g
Tomato two Onion half
garlic three-lobe Potato one
salt one Black pepper Appropriate amount
Tomato sauce Appropriate amount

Tomato beef pasta recipe

Step 1

First prepare the ingredients and boil the water.

Step 2

Pasta is not easy to cook, so according to the requirements of the package to cook for a while, soft taste for the principle of adding some salt and cooking oil to the water.

Step 3

Add butter to a wok. When melted, add onion and minced garlic and stir-fry

Step 4

Add potatoes and a little water, boil it down, and then add tomatoes, otherwise it is not easy to soft rotten.

Step 5

Add beef, boil, add tomato sauce, add black pepper, add according to your taste. Final salt

Step 5

Pour sauce over noodles after cooking.
